About Me

I'm Ivy and I am a

I am a rising sophomore at Dartmouth College double majoring in Computer Science and Economics, and minoring in Environmental Studies. I am interested in the intersection of business and technology and I'm currently exploring management consulting, entrepreneurship and technology management.

In high school, I co-founded two startups and a non-profit, and interned for the LaunchX Entrepreneurship Program in summer 2021. On campus, besides being the Co-Tuck Relations Chair for WIB, I am involved with The Dartmouth Newspaper’s advertising and finance team. In my free time, I enjoy mountain biking, hiking, cooking and spending time with friends and family.

Born in Singapore, and raised in Germany and mainland China before coming to Hong Kong, I am adaptable, resilient, well-sensitized to unfamiliar perspectives, and able to comfortably cross cultural barriers to collaborate with the widest diversity of people. I believe in using cutting-edge technology to address the various needs of the community around me.
